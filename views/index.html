The story of workshop

Concave from skateboard deck is used for making every single pair of Sk8rings sunglasses.
Curvature of nose and tail are used for temples.
First, we cut the shape of sunglasses, there are several models, male and female. Each frame is hand-sanded to the finest detail.
The same is done with temples. For temples and frame we used to choose best fit in variety of colors and patterns.
Varnishing is the final step and we do it in several steps.

SK8RINGS is a Serbian brand that produces sunglasses and jewelry from broken and used skateboard decks. Riding skateboard over 10 years and the whole skate culture gave an idea for recycling. The solution is primarily ecological and fashionable. Sunglasses, rings, bracelets, earrings and all other stuff are handmade and represents a unique products. Almost there are no two same products, with identical colors and shape.
